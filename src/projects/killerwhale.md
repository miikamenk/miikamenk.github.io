A really cool custom keyboard design that's split into multiple different pcb's and connected with FFC connectors.
It's also modular, you can choose between the following thumb clusters for each side:

- joystick
- dpad
- trackball
- scroll wheel

designed by [Tarohayashi](https://tarohayashi.booth.pm/items)
[Killerwhale github](github.com/Taro-Hayashi/KillerWhale)
